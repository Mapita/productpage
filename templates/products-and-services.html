Products and services

Maptionnaire is a cloud service for map based online surveys. Try it yourself!.

All customers get graphical customization and dedicated support, in particular with geographical information systems (GIS). Maptionnaire comes with an easy-to-use editor tool for the creation of surveys and analysis of respondent data. In addition, our team has a long background in peer-reviewed survey research and we offer consultation for successful data collection!

Most of our customers choose an annual subscription, which includes an unlimited number of questionnaires. It is also possible to buy the service for a single project of arbitrary length.

Details

  • In addition to maps by big providers such as MapQuest, you may use custom maps: either directly from your WMS server or by uploading them to our server.
  • Maptionnaire offers an in-browser analysis tool for the map markers placed by respondents.Screenshot from the analysis tool. A few example queries one can make with it:
    • Show all markers from a drawbutton "Community asset".
    • Show all markers from drawbuttons named "Community asset" or "Destination of recreation" and made by respondents who are over 70 years old, female, and who do not have a university degree.
    • Show responses to the follow up question "Tell us more about this place" related markers located within chosen boundaries. (Markers may be filtered as above).
  • Maptionnaire draws quite a few charts with a single click. Example of a chart
  • We pay close attention to the usability of the response data: all responses come in a self-explanatory form without separate meta files. Also, should you have a questionnaire in multiple languages, response data is translated and combined into a single language (except for open answers).
  • Responses are saved in real time, so you will be able to monitor respondent behaviour even if they do not finish the survey.
