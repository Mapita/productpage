{% trans "The story of Mapita and softGIS. From sticker maps to cloud-based Maptionnaire" %}

{% trans "SoftGIS is a research method that examines the experimental knowledge of people in connection with location information." %} {% trans "The word ’soft’ emphasized the human perspective at a time when location information (GIS) was rarely used outside natural sciences." %}

{% trans "Valuable information by simple methods (1995—2003)" %}

{% trans "In the 1990’s, young researcher Marketta Kyttä invited people to workshops to place emotion stickers on maps." %} {% trans "The maps showed clearly how positive or negative experiences abounded in certain places." %}

{% trans "This marked the beginning of the development of the softGIS method." %} {% trans "It turned out to be highly valuable for e.g. architects, who appreciate a map revealing the views of locals about the area they are planning." %} {% trans "SoftGIS helped the planners to understand the qualities of areas from both local and larger perspective." %}

{% trans "The birth of web-based map questionnaires (2004—2009)" %}

{% trans "In 2004, a research project on the experienced quality of life took place in Järvenpää." %} {% trans "For the first time, a web questionnaire was integrated in a map." %} {% trans "The researchers Kyttä and Maarit Kahila were very happy to receive the coordinates directly in a digital form." %} {% trans "The respondents could answer the questionnaire easily on their home computers." %} {% trans "For the researchers managed to collect and analyze a vast response material." %}

{% trans "The following years saw the intense development of web-based map questionnaires." %} {% trans "We improved them to be used in research and planning." %} {% trans "We learned and succeeded. Now map surveys have been carried out in 6 continents." %} {% trans "We have published lots of peer-reviewed research papers, and Marketta Kyttä, the mother of the softGIS method, has become the professor of land use planning." %} {% trans "Anna Broberg has developed methods of analyzing experimental location information." %}

{% trans "In participatory planning, sofGIS opened a new operating model." %} {% trans "The residents could voice their opinions by answering a questionnaire online, without attending a hearing event." %} {% trans "SoftGIS complemented the traditional methods and made participation possible for thousands of people." %}

{% trans "Mapita Inc. is founded to answer the need for softGIS solutions (2010— )" %}

{% trans "We founded Mapita Inc in 2010, because there was a strong demand for sofGIS research in the municipalities of Finland." %} {% trans "We also started to develop our technology to produce a make it yourself survey." %} {% blocktrans %}The result can be found on the pages of Maptionnaire.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "We have various clients and partners in many countries." %} {% trans "Some of commission the whole research from us, which means that they benefit from all of our expertise." %} {% trans "Others use our technology, but want to build the surveys themselves." %}
